Welcome to SoFloFrisbee.co

This project is still way in development. The ultimate goal of the project will be to have a one stop shop to know and participate with all the local frisbee in South Florida, all being done automatically by the application.

The planned features are:


Find Local Frisbee

Say when you're available & find by zip code, and be able to easily add yourself to the ultimate groups that work for you.

Explore Tournaments

Have an easy place to access and find information on the many tournaments happening in the future.

Talk to teams

Get the contact information for college, masters, and youth teams in the local area.


Done Automatically

This app will consume facebook page, text message, and email data automatically from all the pickup groups so no one has to spend time posting on this platform.

Integrate to Google Calendar

Goal will be to parse all the data and let you be able to just add pickup groups to your calendar (and you can find out if they change times)

Whatever you think!
